Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Suggestions Blocker

Effective Date: January 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes the information practices for the LinkedIn Suggestions Blocker browser extension ("the Extension"). Please read this policy carefully to understand how your information is handled when you use the Extension.

What is personal information?

Personal information means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person ("data subject"). Examples include names, email addresses, or identifiers like IP addresses.

Information LinkedIn Suggestions Blocker collects

Personal information The Extension does not collect, process, or store any personal information. No user data, including browsing activity, preferences, or interactions with LinkedIn, is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Anonymous information The Extension does not collect or transmit anonymous usage information. All functionality operates entirely on your local device and does not interact with external systems.

Server logs The Extension does not require server communication, so no server logs or related data are generated or stored.

Analytics

The Extension does not use any analytics or tracking tools.

How LinkedIn Suggestions Blocker processes and discloses collected information

We don’t collect or store your information Because the Extension does not collect or transmit any data, there is no information to process, disclose, or sell to third parties.

Data security

While the Extension does not collect or store data, its open-source code is available for transparency and audit at: https://github.com/dommyrock/sug-purge.

This ensures that users can verify the Extension’s behavior and confirm that no personal information is collected or processed.

Your rights

Since the Extension does not collect or store personal information, there is no data to access, rectify, or erase. If you have any concerns or questions, you may contact us.

Your continued use of the Suggestions Purge website or browser extension constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any revisions or updates.

Thank you for using LinkedIn Suggestions Blocker! We’re committed to maintaining your privacy and providing a distraction-free LinkedIn experience.
